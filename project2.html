The Festival

This project was part of a cultural initiative organized by LABA in the city of Brescia, aimed at increasing public awareness of LABA and fostering a deeper appreciation for culture, art, and fashion in our city. My colleagues and I were responsible for the project’s communication in its entirety, with my team specifically focusing on the creation of the festival’s web page.

Client's Request

In this case, the client, LABA, wanted to draw inspiration from the concept of creative connections. The idea was to use carefully designed spaces to link various arts and disciplines, with an eye toward the future and the development of young talent and culture. To support this vision, the decision was made to create a simple one-page website that could be reused for future editions of the festival. The page’s primary function was to inform users about the festival's program, including dates and locations for the various events.

Design Process

Our team began the process by carefully considering the website's requirements, focusing on all the elements that needed to be showcased. We also took into account the potential for future updates, including the addition of hidden sections and a gallery for past events. To lay the groundwork, we created medium-fidelity wireframes that helped us visualize the placement of information, design the interface, and plan the animated interactions we wanted to incorporate into the project. With this foundation, we set out to create a straightforward yet captivating interface using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with Bootstrap and GSAP libraries to build the web page. The result was a fully customized and updatable site, which was integrated into the university’s domain with a connected database via WordPress.


Challenges and Resolutions

The project timeline was tight, and the client requested several changes during the development process, which presented both challenges and opportunities for growth. Some of these changes were requested in real-time, coinciding with the festival itself. Despite these pressures, I’m proud to say that my colleagues and I were able to quickly adapt and address each request. This experience not only demonstrated our team’s resilience but also reinforced the importance of staying flexible and open to learning new approaches as challenges arise.
