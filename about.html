About me

Who am I?

I'm Nicoletta, a designer from Italy, and I call myself a multidisciplinary kid.
Before becoming a digital designer, I studied literature and communication.
This background allows me to combine the knowledge and skills I've gained from both fields to create something rare and precious.

Why I do this?

I'm extremely curious and have always loved a good enigma.
I adore finding solutions to problems, tackling challenges, and using my creativity to help people with everyday tasks.

What I do and How?

As a UX and UI designer, I focus on researching and identifying issues and pain points. I conduct interviews with users and field experts to understand where I can make a significant impact.
Being a big fan of usability laws and minimalist design, I strive to simplify designs to ensure everyone can have a pleasant user experience.
