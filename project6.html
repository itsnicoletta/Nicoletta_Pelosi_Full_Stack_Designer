The Brand

Amortherapy is a brand that produces concentrated, long-lasting laundry fragrances. The objective of this project was not to create a new marketing strategy but to refine the existing one through analysis and realignment of the brand’s goals.

The Process

The process began with a thorough examination of the communication strategies used by competing brands. The goal was to identify what makes Amortherapy stand out in order to leverage specific unique selling propositions (USPs) on social media. Following this, the analysis shifted to the brand's own communication style, focusing on maintaining its warm and conversational tone of voice across all brand touchpoints.
Next, I conducted data analysis using tools like Google Trends, Instagram Insights, and TikTok to understand what the brand’s buyer personas were searching for and how to create hooks that would resonate with them. This led to the development of new content categories tailored to current trends and popular topics. For instance, since the strategy was to be implemented during the summer and coincided with the release of "Bridgerton," we identified relevant themes that could be used to engage the audience. For instance, "what is the parfume of Dhapne Bridgerton?"

Content Strategy

The new content categories I created were primarily focused on conversion and engagement. We engaged with the audience by asking which Amortherapy scents they preferred, fostering a sense of trust and loyalty by making the customer feel central to the brand. We also included Q&A sessions to gather feedback on how to improve the fragrances, showing customers that their opinions were valued.


Challenges and Resolutions

Finally, I developed an editorial calendar for June and July, organizing all upcoming posts, including appropriate copy, visuals, and the specific objectives of each post, whether it was to be sponsored or not. This planning ensured that we stayed in close contact with the client and that each post received their approval. The editorial calendar was managed through Google Sheets, a highly effective tool for sharing information and keeping the client informed and involved.
