The Challenge

This project was completed in a single day as part of a competition. The prompt was to create a website for the Department of Agriculture of Sri Lanka, with the unique selling proposition of encouraging farmers to adopt more eco-friendly farming practices and join a consortium to access lower prices for supplies and machinery. The primary requirement was to design a registration system that would send relevant information to farmers who signed up.

Design and Implementation

For the design, I chose to draw inspiration from glassmorphism to create a clean and simple interface. The calls to action were made highly visible, and the content was kept minimal yet clear to ensure ease of use.
Given the constraints of the competition, I developed the website from scratch using pure code, without relying on frameworks like Bootstrap. This added challenge required careful attention to detail, particularly since the site would potentially be integrated with a backend system if selected by the department.
